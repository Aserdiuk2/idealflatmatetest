What we do

We advise

Give landlords like you expert advice and guidance on everything from loss of rental income to dealing with problem tenants.

We lobby

Lobbying at the governmental level, to impact legislation and ensure the fair treatment of landlords and their assets.

We connect

Bring together single property landlords with large multi site landlords to share ideals and drive future development.

Benefits of joining the NLA

Full membership offers you:

  • Unlimited access to our expert Telephone Advice Line
  • Discounted rates for our landlord development courses
  • Subscriptions to UK landlord, the premier magazine for landlords
  • Free Tax Investigation Insurance (worth over £100)
